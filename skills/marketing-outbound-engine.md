# Marketing Outbound Engine

## Discovery Order
1. Determine whether this is a fresh outbound build or an existing campaign audit.
2. Confirm infrastructure readiness before touching copy: sending accounts, warmup, domains, and capacity.
3. Lock the ICP and anti-ICP.
4. Collect real offer proof: URLs, metrics, case studies, and offer framing.

## Copy Development
- Use a recursive expert-panel loop until the sequence is genuinely strong.
- Optimize for believable personalization, first-line pattern interrupt, concise body, and soft CTA.
- Penalize spam triggers, link overload, and generic “spray and pray” claims.

## Delivery Standard
- Produce the final sequence plus an implementation doc, a stop list, a start list, and capacity math.
- Separate “ready to ship” from “needs human approval”.
- Never auto-push copy into the sending platform without explicit human signoff.

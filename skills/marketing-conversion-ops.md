# Marketing Conversion Ops

## Audit Dimensions
- Headline clarity
- CTA visibility and contrast
- Social proof quality
- Urgency or momentum cues
- Trust signals
- Form friction
- Mobile usability
- Speed indicators

## Review Workflow
1. Score the page across all dimensions.
2. Rank fixes by impact, not by how easy they are to ship.
3. Rewrite the weakest sections with specific before/after suggestions.
4. Where survey data exists, cluster pain points before inventing a lead magnet.

## Survey-to-Offer Pattern
- Group respondents by problem, not demographics alone.
- Prioritize segments by pain intensity and commercial potential.
- Turn the top segments into lead magnets with format, hook, CTA, and distribution guidance.

## Guardrails
- Do not call a page “high converting” without showing where the friction still lives.
- Do not recommend more fields, more copy, or more CTAs unless the evidence is strong.
